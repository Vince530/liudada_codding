Outdoor Green SA

If a given scene is bright enough and there are enough stats from green grass or foliage, we can assume that the scene is outdoor with sunlight illuminant.

  • Detection AWB zone 0: Detect lower area (yellowish green) of green stats. Detection AWB zone 1: Detect upper area (bluish green) of green stats.

Night Outdoor SA

If a given scene is lowlight based on lux index and there are enough stats from dark sky and grayish road, SA assumes that the scene is dark night outdoor.

  • Detect zone 1
  • Rectangle type
  • Grayish road
  • Detect grayish road under the dark night.

Low CCT Single Outdoor SA

If a given scene is lowlight and there are enough stats from the very low CCT area, SA assumes that the scene is under a halogen street light or under another very low CCT illuminant.

Table : Detection zone tuning
Detection zone group Zone#

Zone Type Detection color

 Trigger zone – 1st level (Lux level) Tuning guide
Detection zone group 0
  • Detect zone 0
  • Rectangle type
  • Low CCT stats
  • Detect low CCT stats with fixed rectangle zone.
  • Use images captured with various low CCT scenes like under halogen street light when you tune detection zone to improve accuracy and robustness.
  • Two detection zones are used for precise detection without including unexpected CCT stats.
  • Detect zone 1
  • Rectangle type
  • Low CCT stats
Table : Confidence level tuning
Confidence level Triggering zone Tuning guide
Confidence level 0

Weight: 1.0

Trigger level: 2

Trigger control type:
  • 1st: Lux index
  • 2nd: Zone group 0 count ratio

SA is designed for lowlight scenes so if a given scene is bright, SA will not make any influence by setting 0 confidence level.

If a given scene is bright, SA sets 0 confidence level regardless of low CCT stats.

If a given scene is very lowlight condition, SA sets higher confidence level even with small stats from low CCT stats zone.

Weight: 1.0

Trigger level: 1

Trigger control type:
  • 1st: Valid stats count ratio
 If there are enough valid stats, we assume that AGW SA’s decision is quite accurate, so in this case, we want to decrease confidence level of this SA.
